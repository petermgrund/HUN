About HÚV

HÚV is Hverland's national public broadcasting corporation.

Our goals:

  • Strengthen Hverlandic democracy
  • Protect Hverlandic and Hverfolk culture
  • Bring people together.
  • Provide high-quality, diverse programming.
A world-class creative and courageous community.
