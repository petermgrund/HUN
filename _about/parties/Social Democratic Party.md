The **Social Democratic Party** (Sosial demokratsiq qat) is a social democratic and techno-realist political party in Hverland.

### Ideology

The Social Democratic Party adheres to left-wing, anti-austerity, and pro-labour policies. The party is considered a techno-realist party, but has a few members who are part of the [Futurist Cooperative](/HUN/about/party/future).
