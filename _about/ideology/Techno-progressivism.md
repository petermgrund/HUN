The **techno-progressive camp** (teknologisiiariarusiut) is a political alignment in Hverland for those who subscribe to [techno-progressivism](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Techno-progressivism). The camp is affiliated with Humanity+, an NGO that supports the "ethical use of technology and evidence-based science to expand human capabilities". The term "techno-progressive" is regularly in use to label the broader segment of the political spectrum that combines a progressive outlook with an optimistic attitude towards technology.
