**Eirsnyn** is a neighborhood in [Tyrvhuln](/HUN/about/locations/tyrvhuln). It is located in the northeastern part of the city. It covers the entire Qaq Peninsula. The neighborhood is known for its high-end residential areas. The neighborhood has an elongated, irregular shape. It tapers towards the east and widens slightly towards the center.

### Demographics
In 2022, 10,015 people lived in Eirsnyn. The neighborhood is mostly ethnically Hverlander, with 9,409 (94%) residents identifying as such. The remaining 5% (506) have a foreign background. The largest foreign groups are from the United States, Iceland, and Germany. The neighborhood has the fewest number of Jallaq residents in the city, who make up less than 1% of the population.

Eirsnyn is the most affluent neighborhood in Tyrvhuln. The median household income in the neighborhood is 1.5 times the city average. The neighborhood has the highest percentage of adult residents with a college degree or higher, at 70%.
