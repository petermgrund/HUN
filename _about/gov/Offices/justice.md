The **Office for Justice and Internal Affairs** is the cabinet-level office responsible for justice and internal affairs policy in Hverland. The office is headed by the Intendant for Justice. The Office for Justice and Internal Affairs is responsible for the administration of the Hverland justice system, including the courts, prisons, and law enforcement agencies.
